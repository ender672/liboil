* For more accurate aspect-ratio preserving scaling, use the following reverse mapping function:
  1. Calculate both x-scale & y-scale inverse scale factors, in/out.
  2. Use the smaller scale factor of the two. Use this factor for both x-scale & 
     y-scale.
  3. Let's call the dimension that adopts the other dimension's scale the
     "trimmed dimension".
  3. Center the source coordinate grid in the trimmed dimension with the
     following algorithm:
       reverse_map = (x + 0.5) * adopted_scale_factor - 0.5 + (trimmed_dimension - non_trimmed_dimension) / 2
